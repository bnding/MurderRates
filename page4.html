This bar graph depicts the frequency of various weapons used to commit the crimes based on the perpetrators’ relationships with the victims. Interestingly, boyfriend, girlfriend, and friend seem to be highest relationships involved in crimes. For crimes committed involving handguns, friends were involved with more crimes, but following closely behind are girlfriends. Murders using blunt objects as weapons are where boyfriend relationships shows the peak for this particular relationship. In general, friends, boyfriends, and girlfriends have been involved with many crimes more than the other relationships.

This stacked bar graph represents the amount of different types of weapons used based on the perpetrator’s sex. The types of sex we have included based on the data set are male and female. In general, the male sex seems to have committed more crimes than females and they have committed the most amount of crimes with a particular types of weapon than the other gender. The weapon most used with the top number of crimes is a handgun. Handguns have approximately been used in more than 200,000 crimes over these years.
